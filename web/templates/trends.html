How to read this page

  • Price ladder — #1 pick list price per model, row by row. Scan down to see whether the best deal is getting cheaper or scarcer.
  • Daily timeline — each day’s top five per model with median reference and day-over-day change on the #1 pick.
  • Model filter — focus one SUV when you are close to buying; use All models for a market-wide pulse.
  • After a week or two of daily snapshots you should see typical price bands, which models have thin inventory (<5 picks), and where deals cluster.
  • Listing links are checked live when you open this page. Sold vehicles are labelled; days in top picks counts from the first to last day we featured that car.
